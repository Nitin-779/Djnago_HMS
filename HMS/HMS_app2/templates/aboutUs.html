About Ghar Ka Khana

Home-cooked happiness, delivered fresh to your hostel or doorstep every single day.

Ghar Ka Khana is a passion project created to solve a very real problem – lack of access to fresh, nutritious, and homely meals for people living away from home.

We understand the craving for "Ghar ka khana", and that’s why we deliver lovingly prepared meals that remind you of home. Our daily menus are carefully curated and cooked with hygiene, heart, and simplicity – just like your mom would make.

Whether you're a student, professional, or just someone who’s tired of unhealthy options, we’ve got your back. We offer affordable daily and monthly plans for breakfast, lunch, and dinner with timely doorstep delivery.

Join our growing family and experience food that brings comfort and joy to your everyday life.
